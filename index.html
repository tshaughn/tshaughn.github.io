Problem statement: Trouble finding mentors

Students and young adults have a hard time finding mentors and getting advice from people in the industry they want to go in. Getting connections and networking is hard for many students. Which makes it hard for them to find internships and research opportunites.

Team Personas: Mentoring Customers

My individual likes to play a lot of tennis and goes to the gym in his free time. He values the idea of working hard to get to his goals in both athletics and academics. He looks forward to having good mentors that will challenge him, while also making him learn well.

Team Storyboards: Customer Scenarios

In this scenario, Dylan is struggling with calculus. To try solving this, he goes on the internet, and finds our mentor app. He then schedules an appointment with a calculus mentor named Jessica. They meet up and she helps him with his calculus. At the end, he gets an A+ because he can finally succeed in calculus.

Team Sketches: Mentor App Pages

My sketch features a customer looking through the different paths of being mentored. My customer picked Math since he is needing a Calculus tutor. My customer then hits the Calculus I option. This brings up a screen that shows him the different mentor that he can choose from. He can then see each mentor's profile. He can then also chat with his mentor to schedule an appointment.
